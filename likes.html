Books

I do enjoy reading non-fiction literature, be it tech, history, economics or sociology. However, all of my favourite books are fiction.

Asimov's Foundation is a remarkable story about people trying to preserve all the best that the human civilisation has to offer from getting lost after the inevitable fall of the galactic empire.
Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality is my favourite novel and possibly the best fan-fiction that has ever been written. The main character is armed with scientific method and is on the path to either save or destroy the world.
Jack London has a great talent for writing stories with truly alive characters. Martin Eden is a passionate and kind person who dedicates himself to education to rise above his surroundings and achieve success. The whole world will be against him...
